FAQs Related to Pharmaceutical Drugs

How do depressant drugs affect?

Depressant drugs work to slowdown the activity in the central nervous system and the body feels relaxed. These are prescription drugs intended to relieve depression, anxiety and stress. 

Why are prescription drugs better?

Prescription drugs are prescribed by medically-trained doctors and physicians who prescribe the medicines to patients after due process of examination and tests. They also consider many aspects of patients, their health conditions, their age, and previous sickness history. All these help them to prescribe the best medicines.

What is expectorant?

Expectorant is a medicine that stimulates the flow of saliva and promotes coughing to get the phlegm out from the respiratory tract.

What is the use of Levozimed?

This pharmaceutical drug is used to treat heart failure. After administration, this medicine increases the pumping activity of the heart and relaxes the blood vessels.

What does Natdac 60 do?

Natdac 60 tablet is an anti-viral medicine, which is used for treating the chronic hepatitis C virus infection.
